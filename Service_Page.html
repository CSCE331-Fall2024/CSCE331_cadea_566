Service

When it comes to community service and volunteer work, I would consider myself pretty generous with an estimated more than 300 hour history of service. Whether is was enforced by my robotics team to do 20 or more hours of community service prior to a season or I chose to do some volunteer work myself. As previously mentioned, all members of my robotics team had to complete atleast 20 hours of community service work to participate in each season. This has led to me taking part in some initiatives such as the PB&J Project where I got to work with the restaurant chain Which Wich to make PB&J sandwiches for those in need. Aside from big team initiatives like the PB&J Project, I also worked at my local food bank in order to continue filling my hours separately on my own time.
